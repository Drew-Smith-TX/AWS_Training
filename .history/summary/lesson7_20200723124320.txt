Cloud Service Models
    - Infrastruce as a Service (Iaas)
    - Platform as a Service(PaaS)
    - Software as a Service (SaaS)
Software as a Service   
    - Delivery of an application that can be widely accessed and distributed
    - Install not needed, accessed via connection
Platform as a Service
    - access to a framework from the operating system and up
    - host hardware, network component, and OS are typically managed, maintained
      and supported by the vendor
    - Great deployment Service for developers
Infrastruce as a Service
    - architect your own portion of the cloud, by configuring a virtual network
    - segmented from other networks allowing you to deploy resources as needed
    - configure instances from the OS and up
Disaster Recovery as a Service
Communications as a Service
Montioring as a service
