Cloud Deployment Models
    Public Cloud
        -vendor makes available to the use of shared infrastructure including:
            - Compute, Storage, Database, and Network Resources
        -Access is provided via internet connection
    Private Cloud
        -hosted, managed, and owned by the company using internet
        - improved and more direct control of company Database
        - tighter control of security
        - Cloud principles are applied to the design
        - Greater capital expenditure is required
    Hybrid Cloud
        - Makes use of both private and public clouds
        - used for seasonal bursts
        - established when a network link is configured between the private clouds
          to services within the public clouds
        - allows you to architect services in the most appropriate Models
        - Normally short term, may be used for test/dev purposes and can often
          be a transitional state for enterprises
